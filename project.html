Rain or Shine : Google Spotlight Stories

We produced the music and sound for
the latest Google Spotlight Story:
‘Rain or Shine’.

We have recently completed the score and sound for Nexus director Felix Massie’s VR Google Spotlight film, Rain or Shine.This is Nexus’ new 360° VR film project coming out later this year in Google’s Spotlight Stories. MORE
